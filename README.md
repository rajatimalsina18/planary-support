## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Why didn’t my tasks appear?  
Make sure they are assigned to a date or imported from Everyday tasks.

Can I edit a task after creating it?  
Yes — tap the task to edit its details.

How do I schedule tasks?  
Drag tasks into the Schedule view or assign a time when creating them.
